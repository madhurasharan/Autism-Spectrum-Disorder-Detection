About Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental disorder that affects communication and behavior. Early detection is crucial for effective intervention and support.

Importance of Early Detection

Early diagnosis of ASD can significantly improve outcomes by enabling timely therapies and support tailored to individual needs.

Methodology

This project uses a machine learning approach based on the XGBoost model. Data collection involved behavioral and demographic features, followed by preprocessing and model training to predict the likelihood of ASD.
